About the Founder

TreVon Hines is a motivated and academically accomplished graduate of the University of South Florida, holding a major in finance and a minor in management, with a 3.87 GPA. He specializes as an analyst with strategic and critical thinking, innovative ideas, and exceptional Excel expertise.

He currently serves as Procurement Representative Associate at Lockheed Martin, following his role as College Intern at Circle K in Tampa, Florida, where he aided finance, fuel, and marketing departments by tracking pump down times, speeding weekly finance reports, creating distribution reports for sales predictions to prevent out-of-stocks, and pulling sales data for profitability actions.

Previously, he worked as Store Associate at CVS Health in Tampa, College Financial Representative at Northwestern Mutual in Greater Tampa Bay Area, and Unit Leader at The Salvation Army in Bloomingdale, New Jersey. These roles honed skills in analyzing information, attention to detail, unloading, mathematics, financial policies, and youth mentoring. He recognizes trends and patterns in market data to maximize margin and win customers. Involved in The National Society of Leadership and Success, Beta Gamma Sigma, and NABA, enhancing leadership and teamwork.

TreVon aims to become CEO of a company and make a positive global impact.
